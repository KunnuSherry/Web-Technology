About Meerut

Meerut is one of the most historic cities of India, famously known for its contribution to the First War of Independence in 1857. Today, Meerut is a major center for sports goods manufacturing, education, and rapid urban development, while still preserving its cultural and historical roots.

Famous Places in Meerut

Augarnath Temple

A highly revered temple linked with the 1857 revolt.

St. John's Church

A historic church built in 1819 showcasing British-era architecture.

Hastinapur

An ancient city mentioned in the Mahabharata and a pilgrimage site.

Suraj Kund

An 18th-century reservoir known for peace and heritage.

Shaheed Smarak

A memorial honoring the heroes of the 1857 freedom struggle.
